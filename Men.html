Catty & Doggy Talking About Crypto

Let's learn about crypto in a fun way!

Dialogue between Cetty and Doggy

Cetty: "Hey Doggy, do you know what crypto is?"

Doggy: "Hmm, crypto? Is that something I can eat?"

Cetty: "Haha, no, Doggy! Crypto is digital money. It means this money only exists on the internet, you can’t hold it like cash."

Doggy: "Oh, so I can’t carry it in my pocket?"

Cetty: "Exactly! But you can store crypto in a digital wallet. And every transaction is recorded on an advanced technology called blockchain."

Doggy: "Blockchain? Like a chain of bones?"

Cetty: "Haha, kind of! So, every transaction is like a link in the chain, and everyone can see the chain, making it safe from fraud."

Doggy: "Wow, that’s cool! I think I want to learn more about crypto. Maybe I can buy digital bones!"

Cetty: "Haha, good idea, Doggy! Welcome to the world of crypto!"
